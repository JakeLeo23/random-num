Rules:

A random number between 1 and 100 is generated. Your job is to try to guess the exact number generated. Enter your guess into the input bar and click enter, If you are between 100 - 40 away from the random number you are cold. If you're between 39 - 20 away from the random number you are luke warm. If you're between 19 - 10 away from the random number you are warm. If you're between 9 - 5 away from the random number you are hot. If you're between 4 - 1 away from the random number you are burning. Upon guessing the random number, you are taken to a victory screen.
